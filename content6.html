About this project

Designed wireframes, prototypes, and mockups using Figma, and implemented UI/UX designs for a profile management page in Android Studio using XML and Kotlin. Developed two views, "View Profile" and "Edit Profile," allowing users to update personal data and profile photos. Applied HCI principles to enhance accessibility and collaborated with the development team to ensure seamless design integration..
